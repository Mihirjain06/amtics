Transport Facility

AMTICS provides safe, reliable, and punctual transportation services to ensure students and faculty have hassle-free commutes from nearby towns and villages. Our buses follow fixed routes and are equipped with tracking and emergency facilities.

Route 1: Valsad → AMTICS

Stops at Tithal, Atul, Pardi, and college campus.

Departs: 7:30 AM
Route 2: Vapi → AMTICS

Stops at Chanod, Dungra, and other local areas en route.

Departs: 7:15 AM
Route 3: Dharampur → AMTICS

Covers Waghai, Nana Pondha, and adjoining areas.

Departs: 6:50 AM
