About Institute

The Asha M. Tarsadia Institute of Computer Science and Technology, popularly known as AMTICS, a distinguished constituent of Uka Tarsadia University, is recognized as one of the best places for education in Computer Science and Engineering in the South Gujarat region. Situated in the vibrant Maliba Campus, AMTICS is a hub of innovation and excellence, offering seven undergraduate and one postgraduate program that cater to the dynamic and emerging fields of technology, including Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security, and Software Engineering.

At AMTICS, we take pride in providing a best-in-class, state-of-the-art academic infrastructure designed to foster creativity, innovation, and hands-on learning. Our mission is to deliver skill-based, industry-oriented education that prepares students to excel in the fast-evolving technological landscape. We bridge the gap between academia and industry through a curriculum aligned with real-world applications, ensuring to prepare future-ready students.

The institute thrives on a vibrant student culture that encourages collaboration, critical thinking, and holistic development. Under the mentorship of experienced academicians, leading researchers, and seasoned industry professionals, students are nurtured to tackle complex societal and technological challenges with confidence.

Whether you aspire to be a trailblazer in cutting-edge technologies or contribute meaningfully to global advancements, AMTICS offers the ideal environment to realize your potential and achieve excellence.
