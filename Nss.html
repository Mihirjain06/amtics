What is NSS?

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at AMTICS is a platform for students to engage in meaningful community service and promote civic sense. We empower youth to participate in activities that contribute to the development of society, build leadership, and promote national integration.

Key Focus: Cleanliness drives, blood donation camps, rural education initiatives, awareness programs.
