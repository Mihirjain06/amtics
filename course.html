B.Tech. Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)

About

The B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering at AMTICS offers a cutting-edge curriculum focused on emerging technologies like AI, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, and Software Development. Designed to blend theory with hands-on experience, the program prepares students for industry challenges and innovation, fostering skilled professionals ready to excel globally.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a dynamic learning environment that nurtures creativity, technical expertise, and ethical values, preparing students to lead in the ever-evolving field of computer science and technology.
