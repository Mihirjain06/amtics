About AMTICS

Asha M. Tarsadia Institute of Computer Science and Technology (AMTICS) is a premier institution dedicated to fostering excellence in computer science education. With a focus on innovation and research, AMTICS equips students with cutting-edge skills to thrive in the tech industry.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a dynamic learning environment that nurtures creativity, technical expertise, and ethical values, preparing students to lead in the ever-evolving field of computer science and technology.
